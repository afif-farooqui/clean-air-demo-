Our History

Established in the year 1990, Clean Air Technology in Jalna Road Aurangabad, Maharashtra is a top player in the category AC Repair & Services. Our well-known establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing customers both local and from other parts of Aurangabad Maharashtra. Over the course of Our journey, we have established a firm foothold in the industry. The belief that customer satisfaction is as important as our products and services, have helped our establishment garner a vast base of customers, which continues to grow day by day. Our employs individuals that are dedicated towards their respective roles and put in a lot of effort to achieve the common vision and larger goals of the company.
